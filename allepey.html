Alleppey Boat house

The Keralan town of Alleppey is well-known for its breathtaking backwaters, but it also offers beaches, temples, and traditional boat races. This is a well-liked tourist attraction because of its Ayurvedic spas and wellness centers. Anybody visiting the area will be captivated by the spotless roads, houseboats, coconut trees, and banana leaves. Alappuzha, sometimes called the Arabian Sea coast, is the oldest and most planned backwater area in Kerala. Alleppey, known as the Venice of India, is the top tourist destination in Kerala because of its tropical village life, houseboats in the backwaters, and its reputation as the Venice of India.
